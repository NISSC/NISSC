Non-Inferiority Trials

Failing to reject the null hypothesis is not sufficient to say two treatments are identical.

Blackwelder, W. C. (1982). "Proving the null hypothesis in clinical trials."

General Hypotheses

  • H0: "The experimental treatment is inferior to the control treatment by more than some pre-determined margin."
  • Ha: "The experimental treatment is either better than the control treatment or the experimental treatment is inferior to the control treatment by less than some pre-determined margin."
