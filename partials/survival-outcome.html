Survival Outcome

Things to enter

  • One-sided significance level: The probability of choosing the alternative hypothesis when the null hypothesis is true (bad decision)
  • Target power: The probability of choosing the alternative hypothesis when the alternative hypothesis is true (good decision)
  • Proportion of experimental group in total sample size (PropC)
  • Proportion of control group in total sample size (PropE)
  • Probability of observing an event in experimental group (pC)
  • Probability of observing an event in control group (pE)
  • Non-inferiority margin: "the largest difference that is clinically acceptable"

Reference

S.C. Chow, H. Wang, and J. Shao, Sample Size Calculations in Clinical Research, Second Edition. CRC Press, 2007, p. 174-179
